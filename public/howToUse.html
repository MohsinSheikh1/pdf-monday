Exporting Boards and Groups to PDF with PDFXport

Introduction

PDFXport is a powerful tool that seamlessly integrates with monday.com to help you export boards and groups to PDF. With this guide, you'll learn how to use the app to efficiently export your data in just a few simple steps.

Step 1: Accessing PDFXport

  • 1.1. In your monday.com workspace, open the board you want to export or navigate to the group within the board that you wish to export.
  • 1.2. Look for the three dots (ellipses) located next to the group's name. Click on these dots to reveal a menu.
  • 1.3. In the menu, scroll down to find "Apps" and select PDFXport from the list.

Step 2: Exporting a Group to PDF

  • 2.1. After selecting PDFXport, a small window will appear on your screen.
  • 2.2. In this window, you'll see the option to "Export Group" Click on this button.
  • 2.3. Your PDF will be exported.

Step 3: Exporting an Entire Board to PDF

  • 3.1. Follow steps 1.1 and 1.2 to navigate to the board you want to export.
  • 3.2. In the "Apps" menu, select PDFXport.
  • 3.3. In the small window, click on "Export Board"
  • 3.4. Your board will be successfully exported.

Step 4: Scheduling an Export

  • 4.1. To schedule an export, navigate to the board or group you want to schedule using steps 1.1 and 1.2.
  • 4.2. In the "Apps" menu, select PDFXport.
  • 4.3. In the small window, click on "Schedule Export"
  • 4.4. You'll be directed to the Schedule page, where you can choose the date and time for the export and also you will need to provide an email where PDF will be exported to.
  • 4.5. After selecting the date and time and writing email, click either "Schedule Group" or "Schedule Board" to set up the scheduled export.

Conclusion

PDFXport makes exporting monday.com boards and groups to PDF a breeze. Follow these steps to efficiently export your data or schedule exports for future use. If you have any questions or encounter any issues, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team for assistance.
